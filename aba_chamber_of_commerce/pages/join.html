Sponsorship

Aba is a city well known for its industrialization. Business is at the heart of this city. In addition to serving the people, business is still business and handsome returns help determine the success of a business. We love to support businesses which is one of the benefits of joining the Aba chamber of commerce. Sponsorship for especially small and medium sized businesses is one of the benefits of being a part of this chamber of commerce.


Social Media Posts

Every of our members get to see their business posted on our social media accounts of over 5 million followers. Depending on the membership level, you get to have your businesses featured on our pages once for non-profit members, twice for bronze, thrice for silver and five times for gold members.


Newsletter

We promise to keep you updated with business news 24/7 so that you never miss any information or opportunity. The news letter is for all as we love our members so dearly. Isn't this amazing!


Coupon

You have a chance to win a coupon for just being a member of the Aba chamber of commerce. Depending on your membership, you get a level of coupon. Coupons ranging from $200 to $1000 all for grabs to help make this festive period a little more interesting.
